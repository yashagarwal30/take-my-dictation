## Getting Started with Free Trial

### Step 1: Start Your Free Trial

1. Visit the Take My Dictation homepage
2. Click "Try Free - No Signup Required"
3. Enter your email address
4. Click "Start Free Trial"

**What you get:**
- 10 minutes of free recording time
- Instant access (no credit card required)
- Full access to transcription and summary features
- 24-hour trial session

### Step 2: Record Your First Audio

1. After starting your trial, you'll see the recording interface
2. Click "Upload Audio" or use the microphone to record
3. Supported formats: MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, FLAC
4. Maximum file size: 100MB

### Step 3: View Your Transcription

1. Wait for transcription to complete (usually 30-60 seconds)
2. Review the transcribed text
3. Edit if needed

### Step 4: Generate a Summary

1. Choose your summary format:
   - **Quick Summary**: Concise overview
   - **Meeting Notes**: Structured with action items
   - **Product Spec**: Problem, solution, requirements
   - **Minutes of Meeting**: Formal meeting minutes
2. Click "Generate Summary"
3. Download as PDF or Word document

### Trial Limitations

- 10 minutes total recording time
- Summaries are not saved (download immediately!)
- Audio files are deleted after summary generation
- No audio retention or regeneration features

## Converting Trial to Paid Account

### Why Upgrade?

**Unlock these benefits:**
- Save summaries to your dashboard
- 10+ hours of recording time per month
- Audio retention (Pro plan)
- Regenerate summaries in different formats
- Priority support

### How to Convert Your Trial

#### Option 1: During Trial
1. Click "Subscribe" button on recording page
2. Choose your plan (Basic or Pro)
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email (6-digit code)
5. Complete payment via Stripe

#### Option 2: After Trial Expires
1. Go to takemydictation.ai/signup
2. Enter the same email you used for trial
3. Create a password
4. Verify your email
5. Choose your subscription plan
6. Complete payment

**Important:** Your trial usage history is preserved when you convert to a paid account.

## Recording Audio

### Supported Formats
- MP3
- WAV
- M4A
- OGG
- FLAC
- WebM

### Upload Options

**Option 1: Upload Existing File**
1. Click "Upload Audio"
2. Select file from computer
3. Add custom name (optional)
4. Click "Upload"

**Option 2: Record Live (Browser)**
1. Click "Record"
2. Allow microphone access
3. Speak clearly into microphone
4. Click "Stop" when finished
5. Review and upload

### Best Practices

**For Best Transcription Quality:**
- Use a quality microphone
- Minimize background noise
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Keep audio under 2 hours per file
- Use lossless formats (WAV, FLAC) when possible

**Naming Conventions:**
- Use descriptive names: "Q1 Strategy Meeting - Jan 15"
- Include dates for easy reference
- Use consistent naming across recordings

## Generating Summaries

### Summary Formats Explained

#### Quick Summary
**Best for:** Fast overview of content
**Output:** 1-2 paragraphs highlighting main points
**Use cases:**
- Quick review before full meeting
- Email updates to team
- Social media posts

#### Meeting Notes
**Best for:** Team meetings, client calls
**Output:** Structured format with:
- Meeting overview
- Key discussion points
- Action items with owners
- Next steps and deadlines

#### Product Spec
**Best for:** Product discussions, planning sessions
**Output:**
- Problem statement
- Proposed solution
- User stories
- Requirements and acceptance criteria
- Technical considerations

#### Minutes of Meeting (MOM)
**Best for:** Formal meetings, board meetings
**Output:**
- Meeting metadata (date, time, attendees)
- Agenda items
- Decisions made
- Action items
- Next meeting details

### Using Custom Prompts

Add specific instructions:
- "Focus on technical decisions"
- "Highlight budget discussions"
- "Extract only action items"
- "Summarize in bullet points"

## Audio Retention (Pro Users)

### What is Audio Retention?

Pro users can keep original audio files for 10 days after upload, enabling:
- Regenerating summaries in different formats
- Re-transcribing with different settings
- Keeping backup of important recordings

### How It Works

**Automatic:**
- Pro users: Audio kept for 10 days
- Basic users: Audio deleted after first summary
- Trial users: Audio deleted immediately

**Manual Control:**
1. Go to recording details
2. Toggle "Keep Audio"
3. Choose retention period (up to 10 days)
4. Audio deleted automatically after expiry

### Regenerating Summaries

**Requirements:**
- Pro subscription
- Original audio still available (within 10 days)

**Steps:**
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Find the recording
3. Click "Regenerate Summary"
4. Choose new format
5. Generate new summary

**Use cases:**
- Convert meeting notes to product spec
- Create multiple formats from one recording
- Improve summary with better prompts

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Billing

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime from Settings ‚Üí Subscription. Access continues until billing period ends.

**Q: What happens to my data when I cancel?**
A: Summaries are kept for 30 days. After that, they're permanently deleted. Export before canceling!

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, we offer 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently, but you can downgrade to Basic plan.

### Usage & Features

**Q: What counts toward my monthly limit?**
A: Only the duration of uploaded/recorded audio counts. Transcription and summary generation don't count.

**Q: Can I share summaries with my team?**
A: Yes! Export as PDF/Word and share files. Team collaboration features coming soon.

**Q: How accurate is the transcription?**
A: 95%+ accuracy with clear audio. Accuracy depends on audio quality, accents, and background noise.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: Currently English only. More languages coming in 2026.

### Technical

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) and never share data with third parties.

**Q: What's the maximum file size?**
A: 100MB per file. For larger files, split into segments.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! Our web app works on mobile browsers. Native apps coming soon.

**Q: Do you have an API?**
A: Yes! API access available for Pro users. Contact support@takemydictation.ai

### Troubleshooting

**Q: My transcription has errors**
- Try uploading higher quality audio
- Reduce background noise
- Speak more clearly
- Use a better microphone

**Q: Upload fails**
- Check file format (must be MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, FLAC, WebM)
- Ensure file is under 100MB
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser

**Q: Summary is not relevant**
- Try different summary format
- Add custom prompt with specific instructions
- Ensure transcription is accurate first
- Contact support for help

## Tips & Best Practices

### Recording Tips

1. **Test your setup** - Do a 30-second test recording first
2. **Use external mic** - Better quality than laptop mic
3. **Quiet environment** - Reduces transcription errors
4. **Name recordings** - Use descriptive, consistent names
5. **Check levels** - Avoid distortion from loud audio

### Summary Tips

1. **Choose right format** - Match format to use case
2. **Use custom prompts** - Get exactly what you need
3. **Review before exporting** - Edit transcription if needed
4. **Keep originals** - Export and save important summaries
5. **Leverage regeneration** - Pro users can try different formats

### Workflow Optimization

1. **Batch recordings** - Process similar recordings together
2. **Create templates** - Use same custom prompts repeatedly
3. **Set up naming conventions** - Easy to find later
4. **Regular cleanup** - Delete unnecessary recordings
5. **Monitor usage** - Stay within monthly limits
